Three yogurt-based sauces that’ll kick your meals up a notch—tangy, creamy, and fresh. Perfect for veggies, grilled meats, or anything you’re craving. Once you try these, you’ll never settle for basic again.

Mustard Yogurt Sauce – The Tangy Game Changer

Creamy, tangy, and just the right kick! Perfect for salads, grilled veggies, or anything that needs a flavor boost. 🧀🥗

  • 🫙 200g Greek yogurt (or plain yogurt)
  • 🌟 1 tsp mustard
  • 🍋 Squeeze of lemon juice
  • 🧄 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 🫒 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 🧀 50g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 🌿 Dried oregano, to taste
  • 🌶️ Paprika (optional, for a little kick)
Tzatziki Yogurt Sauce - The Fresh, Cool Classic

Packed with cucumber and dill, this is the ultimate dip for veggies, pita, or anything grilled. It’s fresh, creamy, and totally addictive. 🥒🍋

  • 🫙 200g Greek yogurt (or plain yogurt)
  • 🥒 1 cucumber, grated
  • 🧄 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 🍋 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 🌿 A handful of fresh dill, chopped
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 🫒 Olive oil (optional, if the yogurt is too thick)
Tartar Yogurt Sauce – The Seafood Lover’s Dream

A creamy twist on the classic tartar, with dill, capers, and mustard. The perfect partner for fish and fries (or anything crispy!). 🐟🍟

  • 🫙 200g Greek yogurt (or plain yogurt)
  • 🌿 2 tbsp chopped dill
  • 🥒 1 tbsp chopped capers
  • 🍋 Squeeze of half a lemon
  • 🥄 3 tbsp relish
  • 🌟 2 tsp mustard
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper, to taste
Tips & Suggestions:
  • ✨ Add fresh herbs or a little honey for extra flavor!
  • 🎨 Feel free to improvise! I’d love to hear your suggestions and how you put your own spin on this sauce.
Let's Do This:
  • 🥄 Prep the mustard yogurt sauce: Mix yogurt, mustard, garlic, lemon, olive oil, salt, pepper, feta, and oregano. Add paprika for extra heat.
    200g Greek yogurt, 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp olive oil, 50g feta, oregano, paprika (optional)
  • 🥒 Prep the tzatziki yogurt sauce: Grate cucumber, squeeze out the juice, mix with yogurt, garlic, lemon, dill, salt, and pepper. Add olive oil if too thick.
    200g Greek yogurt, 1 cucumber, 1 garlic clove, 1 tbsp lemon juice, dill, olive oil (optional)
  • 🍽️ Prep the yogurt tartar sauce: Mix yogurt, dill, capers, lemon, relish, mustard, salt, and pepper.
    200g Greek yogurt, 2 tbsp dill, 1 tbsp capers, 3 tbsp relish, 2 tsp mustard, salt, pepper
  • ✨ Serve: Enjoy these sauces with veggies, grilled meats, fish, fries, or anything you love!
