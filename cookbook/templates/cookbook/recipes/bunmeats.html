Soft, golden, and packed with perfectly seasoned meat—these buns are pure comfort food! 🤤

Ingredients:

For the Buns:

  • 🍞 2.2lbs (1kg) flour
  • 🍳 2 eggs
  • 💧 2 cups (480ml) water
  • 🌿 1/2 cup (125ml) vegetable oil
  • 🍬 2/3 cup (135g) sugar
  • 🧂 1 tsp salt
  • 🌱 Bun seasoning: sesame seeds, garlic flakes, poppy seeds (or whatever you like!)

For the Meat Filling:

  • 🥩 1lb (500g) minced meat
  • 🧅 2 large onions, chopped
  • 🌿 Handful of chopped parsley
  • 🫒 Olive oil
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper to taste
  • 🌶️ 1 tsp turmeric, paprika, cumin, and your favorite spices
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 Quick Rise: Place dough in a warm oven (50°C / 120°F) for faster rising.
  • 🍴 Sauce Pairing: These are amazing with Chimichurri or Tahini 🙌
Let's Do This:
  • Cook onions in olive oil until golden. Add minced meat and cook through, then stir in spices. Remove from heat and add parsley. Let cool.
    2 large onions, 500g minced meat
  • In a mixer, combine all bun ingredients (except salt). Once fully combined, add salt and mix for another 10 minutes.
    2.2lbs (1kg) flour, 2 eggs, 2 cups (480ml) water, 1/2 cup (125ml) vegetable oil, 2/3 cup (135g) sugar, 1 tsp salt
  • Let dough rise until doubled. (For a faster rise, check the Tips section!)
    Dough rising time
  • Divide dough into balls, flatten, fill with meat, and seal.
    2.8oz (80g) per bun, meat filling
  • Rise: Let buns rest for 20 minutes to rise.
  • Bake: Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Brush with egg wash, top with seasoning, and bake for 35 minutes until golden!
