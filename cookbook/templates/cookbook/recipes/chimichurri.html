This vibrant, zesty sauce is all you need to take your meals to the next level! Perfect for grilled meats, veggies, or even as a dip. 🔥

Ingredients:

  • 🫑 1-2 red + yellow peppers
  • 🧄 3 garlic cloves
  • 🌿 1oz (30g) parsley
  • 🌱 1oz (30g) coriander
  • 🌶️ 1-2 chillis
  • 🧂 1 tsp salt
  • 🧂 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 🫒 1/2 cup (100ml) olive oil
  • 🍷 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 🌿 1 tsp oregano (optional)
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 🔥 Want more heat? Add extra chillis for a fiery kick!
  • 🔪 Don't have a food processor? No problem, just chop everything finely by hand!
Let's Do This:
  • Finely chop or pulse all the ingredients in a food processor until well combined.
    peppers, garlic, parsley, coriander, chillis, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper, 1/2 cup (100ml) olive oil, 2 tbsp red wine vinegar, 1 tsp oregano (optional)
  • Add everything to a bowl, stir, and let it sit for at least 10 minutes to allow those flavors to marry together.
  • Drizzle over your favorite dishes—grilled meat, roasted veggies, or just dip some warm bread in it. You won’t be disappointed! 😍
