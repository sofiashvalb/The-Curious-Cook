I've got to admit, I never expected a fish dish to be so simple and tasty, but this garlic butter salmon is a total game-changer. One bite, and you'll be hooked for good!

Ingredients:

  • 🐟 Salmon fillet
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper (to taste)
  • 🌶️ Paprika (or any spices you like—feel free to experiment!)
  • 🧈 ~1/4 cup (~50g) butter
  • 🧄 2 cloves garlic – Minced
  • 🌿 Fresh dill (or any herbs you'd like)
  • 🍋 Optional: Lemon juice
Tips & Suggestions:
  • ✨ Remember! Add lemon juice only after cooking to keep the garlic from turning green! 😜
  • ✨ The fresher the fish, the better the flavor!
Let's Do This:
  • First, preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) to get it ready for baking.
  • Season the salmon fillet with salt, pepper, and paprika, ensuring it's evenly coated. Then, bake the salmon for 15-20 minutes, or until it's cooked through.
  • While the salmon is baking, melt the butter in the microwave using 30-second pulses. Once melted, add the minced garlic and fresh dill to the butter and mix well.
  • Once the salmon is done baking, pour the garlic butter mixture over the salmon and cook it for an additional 4-5 minutes to infuse the flavors.
    Make sure you cook the salmon last—it's juiciest right out of the oven!
