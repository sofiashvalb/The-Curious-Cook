This zicchini soup is a cozy classic with a twist—it's got carrots in the mix! 🥕 Simple, yet so flavorful. You can skip the carrots, but they definitely boost the taste!

Ingredients:

For the Soup:

  • 🧅 1 onion
  • 🥕 2-3 carrots
  • 🌿 2-3 celery stalks (including leaves for later)
  • 🥒 2-3 zucchini
  • 🧄 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 🌱 Fresh parsley & cilantro
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper to taste
  • 🌶️ Cumin & turmeric (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 🍲 1 cup vegetable broth (optional)
Tip:
  • 🥔 If you like your soups a bit more substantial, adding potatoes can make it richer and more filling. Though I personally prefer a lighter soup, feel free to adjust to your taste!
Let’s Do This:
  • Heat some olive oil in a large pot and sauté the onion, carrots, and celery stalks until softened and lightly caramelized.
  • Add the zucchini, parsley, cilantro, celery leaves, and garlic, cooking for a few more minutes until fragrant.
  • Season with salt, pepper, and optional cumin and turmeric to taste.
  • Pour in the vegetable broth (if using) and enough water to cover the veggies. Let it simmer on medium heat for 40-50 minutes, until everything is soft.
  • Once the carrots are tender, turn off the heat.
  • Blend the soup until smooth, adding more water to reach your preferred consistency.
  • For an extra flavor boost, drizzle with sesame oil and soy sauce before serving! You can also add some dried chili flakes for a little extra heat. Quick, easy and Sooo Goooood!ל
