This slow-roasted beef brisket is packed with bold flavors and topped with a zesty Chimichurri sauce for the perfect finishing touch. Get ready for tender, juicy slices that are sure to satisfy! 🔥

Ingredients:

  • 🍖 2.2 lbs (1 kg) beef brisket
  • 🧂 2 tbsp salt
  • 🌶️ 3 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 🌶️ 2 tbsp paprika
  • 🧄 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 🥄 4 tbsp barbecue sauce
  • 🍯 4 tbsp honey
  • 🥄 2 tbsp mustard
  • 🫒 1/2 cup (120ml) olive oil
  • 💧 1/2 cup (120ml) water
  • 🌿 Feel free to add any other spices or herbs you love!
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 🌶️ Spice things up: Add a pinch of cayenne or chili flakes to the brisket rub for an extra kick!
  • 🍴 Pair it with roasted veggies or mashed potatoes for a complete meal.
Let's Do This:
  • First, combine all the seasonings and sauces. Rub the mixture all over the brisket, cover it, and refrigerate for *24 hours* to let the flavors meld.
  • Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F), then roast the brisket, covered, for 1 hour to start the cooking process.
  • After 1 hour, lower the oven temperature to 120°C (250°F). Moisten the brisket with its own juices and continue cooking for another 3 hours.
  • Once the brisket has cooked for 4 hours, uncover it and broil at 180°C (350°F) for 15 minutes to achieve a crispy, flavorful top. 🍽️
  • Once done, slice the brisket and serve with a Chimichurri sauce on the side for extra flavor.
