Soft, creamy burrata, fresh veggies, and a tangy dressing to bring it all together—this salad is a real crowd-pleaser! ✨

Ingredients:

For the Salad:

  • 🧀 Burrata cheese
  • 🍅 Cherry tomatoes
  • 🥑 1 avocado (cubed)
  • 🌱 Fresh basil leaves
  • 🥬 Arugula leaves
  • 🌿 Optional: Small radishes (thinly sliced)

For the Dressing:

  • 🫒 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 🍷 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 🍋 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 🧄 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper to taste
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 You can let the burrata sit in warm water for a few minutes to soften the inside. It makes the cheese even creamier! 😍
  • 💡 No burrata? Use mozzarella balls ripped apart instead—still delish! 🧀✨
Let's Do This:
  • Place the arugula leaves on a flat plate. Spread the tomatoes (cut in halves or quarters) and avocado cubes. Add radish slices if using.
  • In a separate dish, mix together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, garlic (minced), salt, and pepper. Drizzle the dressing over the salad.
    2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 2 garlic cloves, salt, pepper
  • Place the burrata on top of the salad. If you want to be extra fancy, rip it apart instead of placing it whole, letting that creamy goodness ooze out. 😍
  • Drizzle some of the dressing on top of the burrata.
