Easy, decadent, and a dream for chocolate lovers. 😍 This cake is rich, fudgy, and totally indulgent—with just 4 simple ingredients and ✨NO flour ✨. It’s everything you crave in a dessert. Let’s get baking!

Ingredients:

For the Cake:

  • 🍫 8 oz (225g) dark chocolate
  • 🧈 4 oz (120g) butter
  • 🥚 6 large eggs (separated)
  • 🍯 1/2 cup (4 oz / 100g) sugar
  • 🧂 Pinch of salt
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 Don’t overbake—this cake should be fudgy and soft in the middle. The chill time makes all the difference!
  • 🍫 Bonus Tip: For an extra indulgent touch, pour melted chocolate on top of the cake before serving. Or, simply sprinkle some cocoa powder.
Let’s Do This:
  • Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F), static.
  • In a large bowl, melt the butter and dark chocolate together until smooth. Set aside to cool.
    8 oz (225g) dark chocolate, 4 oz (120g) butter
  • In a mixer, whisk the egg yolks, salt, and sugar until light and fluffy.
    6 large eggs (separated), Pinch of salt, 1/4 cup (50g) sugar
  • Gently fold the egg yolk mixture into the melted chocolate until fully combined.
  • In a clean bowl, beat the egg whites with the remaining sugar until stiff peaks form.
    1/4 cup (50g) sugar
  • Carefully fold the egg whites into the chocolate mixture. Be gentle but make sure it's well mixed.
  • Grease a 22cm (8.5") ring mold with butter (or use a springform pan). Pour in the batter, smooth the top, and bake for 30-35 minutes. The cake should be slightly wobbly in the center.
  • Let the cake cool completely in the fridge for at least 5 hours (trust me, it’s worth the wait!). After cooling, remove from the ring and transfer to a serving plate.
