A festive, spiced masterpiece to celebrate the season! Perfect for cozying up by the fire or toasting with friends and family. Let the holiday celebrations begin! 🎉🎄

Ingredients:

  • 🍊 1 orange, sliced
  • 🍊 Zest of 1 orange
  • 🍷 750ml (25.4oz) red wine (your fave!)
  • 🥃 30ml (1oz) brandy (optional, but so good!)
  • 🧂 3 tbsp (45g) sugar
  • ✨ 3 cinnamon sticks
  • ⭐ 2-3 star anise
  • 🌿 12 cloves
  • 🍊 Juice of 1 orange
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 If you're feeling extra festive, add a few cranberries for color and crunch! 🍒
Let’s Do This:
  • In a pot, add sugar, cinnamon sticks, star anise, and cloves.
    3 tbsp (45g) sugar, 3 cinnamon sticks, 2-3 star anise, 12 cloves
  • Pour in 3-4 tbsp (45-60ml) of wine and let it melt together over low heat.
  • Toss in orange slices, zest, remaining wine, brandy (if using), and fresh orange juice.
    1 orange, sliced, zest of 1 orange, 750ml (25.4oz) red wine, 30ml (1oz) brandy (optional), juice of 1 orange
  • Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes—smell that holiday magic!
  • Pour into mugs, garnish with a cinnamon stick or orange slice, and sip away!
