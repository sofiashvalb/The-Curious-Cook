Made this Lemon Key Pie on a whim, and now I can't stop eating it. The tangy lime filling and buttery crust are the perfect combination. I'm definitely not sorry about it.

Ingredients:

For the Crust:

  • 🍪 1 cup (125g) Graham crackers (or any biscuits you have)
  • 🧈 6 tbsp (84g) melted butter
  • 🍬 2 tbsp (30g) brown sugar

For the Filling:

  • 🥚 4 eggs
  • 🍋 1/3 cup (80ml) fresh lime juice
  • 🍋 Zest of 2 limes
  • 🥛 1 can (375g) sweetened condensed milk

For Decoration:

  • 🍦 1 cup (250ml) heavy cream (whipping cream)
  • 🍰 1 package (80g) instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 🍋 Lime zest (for topping)
  • 🌰 Optional: Crushed pistachios or fresh raspberries for garnish
Tips & Suggestions:
  • ✨ Can’t find limes? No worries, lemons are great too!
  • ✨ Want a crunchy twist? Throw in some crushed pistachios in the crust with the biscuits.
  • ✨ You can make the cream lighter by adding milk. No piping, but the spread is crazy delicious
Let’s Do This:
  • Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F).
  • Crush crackers, mix with melted butter and brown sugar until it resembles wet sand.
    1 1/2 cups (150g) graham crackers, 1/2 cup (113g) melted butter, 2 tbsp (30g) brown sugar
  • Press the mixture into a pie dish, bake for 15 minutes until slightly golden, and allow it to cool.
  • In a mixing bowl, whisk egg yolks, whole egg, 1 can sweetened condensed milk, fresh lime juice, and lime zest until smooth.
    3 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, can (375g) of sweetened condensed milk, 1/3 cup (80ml) fresh lime juice, zest of 2 limes
  • Beat 3 egg whites until stiff peaks form, then gently fold them into the lime filling mixture until well combined.
  • Pour the lime filling into the cooled crust and bake for another 15 minutes, or until the filling is set but still slightly jiggly.
  • Let the pie cool to room temperature, then chill it in the fridge for at least 6 hours. For the best results, chill overnight.

For Decoration:

  • Whip heavy cream with instant vanilla pudding mix.
    1 cup (250ml) heavy cream, package (80g) instant vanilla pudding mix
  • Slather or pipe it on the pie, then top with lime zest, pistachios, or any fresh berries! 🍋🍓
