This easy-to-make ice cream recipe is a delicious and easy way to satisfy your sweet tooth without an ice cream maker. What's your favorite ice cream flavor? Don't be afraid to improvise.🍓🍫

Ingredients:

For the Ice Cream:

  • 🍶 16.9 fl oz (500ml) double cream (or heavy cream) - Chill before whipping
  • 🥛 1 can (397g / 14oz) condensed milk
  • 🍦 Your favorite flavorings (vanilla, chocolate, nuts, etc.)
Tips & Suggestions:
  • ⏱️ Want it faster? Freeze a smaller amount of ice cream in a smaller container for quicker results.
  • 🎨 Experiment with flavors: Create your own unique ice cream combinations (chocolate chips, nuts, berries, etc.)!
Let's Do This:
  • 🥄 Beat the double cream and condensed milk together until the mixture is thick, smooth, and well combined.
    16.9 fl oz (500ml) double cream, 1 can (397g / 14oz) condensed milk
  • 🍫 Add your favorite flavorings or mix-ins (like chocolate chips, vanilla extract, or fruit) and gently fold them in to keep the texture light.
  • ❄️ Transfer the mixture into a container, cover, and freeze for a minimum of 6 hours, or until it's firm and scoopable.
    Use a container with a capacity of at least 1 liter
  • 🍦 Scoop out the ice cream, drizzle with chocolate sauce or your topping of choice, and enjoy a creamy, homemade treat!
    Toppings: as desired (chocolate sauce, caramel, sprinkles)
