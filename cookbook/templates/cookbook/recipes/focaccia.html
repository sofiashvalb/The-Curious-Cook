No fancy equipment required—just your oven, a little patience, and a dough folding technique to create that perfect airy texture. This focaccia will melt in your mouth!

Ingredients:

For the Focaccia:

  • 💧 1 1/3 cups (320g) room temp. water
  • 🧑‍🍳 1 tsp (3g) dry yeast
  • 🧂 1/2 tsp (10g) salt
  • 🫒 1 tbsp (13g) olive oil
  • 🌾 2 3/4 cups (400g) focaccia/pizza flour

Optional Topping:

  • 🧂 Coarse salt to taste
  • 🫒 A drizzle of olive oil
  • 🌿 Fresh rosemary (optional)
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 Get creative with the toppings! You can add any ingredients you love—cheese, cherry tomatoes, olives, garlic, or even a sprinkle of herbs. The possibilities are endless!
  • 🍞 For extra flavor, drizzle a little more olive oil on top before baking, and don’t be shy with the salt!
Let’s Do This:
  • In a bowl, mix together water, yeast, salt, and olive oil.
    1 1/3 cups (320g) room temp. water, 1 tsp (3g) dry yeast, 1/2 tsp (10g) salt, 1 tbsp (13g) olive oil
  • Gradually add flour and stir until you get a sticky, smooth dough.
    2 3/4 cups (400g) focaccia/pizza flour
  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, then drape a towel over it. Set a timer for 30 minutes.
  • After 30 minutes, dampen your hands and gently fold the dough from the edges toward the center. Cover and let it rest for another 30 minutes.
  • Repeat the folding process every 30 minutes for 4 more rounds using damp hands.
  • Once the dough is ready, grease your pan lightly with olive oil and gently transfer the dough. Let it rise for another 20 minutes.
  • Press down gently on the dough with your fingers to create dimples and bubbles. Drizzle a bit more olive oil on top and sprinkle with salt.
  • Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C) and bake for 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
  • Take it out of the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes before slicing and enjoying!
