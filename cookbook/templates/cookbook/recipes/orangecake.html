This whole orange cake is a game changer! 🍊 It’s incredibly soft, bursting with flavor, and hands down one of the best orange cakes I’ve ever made—using the entire orange, peel and all! You’ll definitely want this recipe in your collection! 👇

Ingredients:

For the Cake:

  • 🍊 1 whole medium-soft orange
  • 🍚 1 cup (7 oz / 200g) sugar
  • 🛢️ 1/2 cup (4 fl oz / 120ml) vegetable oil
  • 🥚 3 eggs
  • 🍦 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 🌾 1 cup (5 oz / 140g) flour
  • 🧁 2 tsp baking powder
  • 🧂 1/4 tsp salt

For the Topping (Optional):

  • 🍬 100g powdered sugar
  • 🌰 Sliced toasted almonds
Let’s Do This:
  • Wash the orange thoroughly, cut off the top, and chop the rest (including the peel) into cubes. Remove any seeds.
  • In a food processor, combine the chopped orange, eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla. Blend until smooth.
    1 whole orange, 3 eggs, 1 cup (200g) sugar, 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil, 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Add flour, baking powder, and salt. Pulse a few times until everything is well combined.
    1 cup (140g) flour, 2 tsp baking powder, 1/4 tsp salt
  • Pour the batter into a greased (or parchment-lined) 20x28 cm (8x11 inch) baking pan. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for about 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Avoid overbaking!
  • Let the cake cool slightly, then top with toasted almonds and dust with powdered sugar (optional). Enjoy! 😋
