Chewy, rich, and SO delicious—you won't be able to stop at just one! 🍫🍪 Quick and easy, these brownies are bound to become your new favorite treat! 😍

Ingredients:

For the Brownies:

  • 🧈 2/3 cup (150g) butter
  • 🍬 1/3 cup (67g) brown sugar
  • 🌾 2 cups (160g) quick oats
  • 🍫 7oz (200g) dark chocolate
  • 🥚 2 medium eggs
  • 🧂 A pinch of salt
  • 🧁 1/2 tsp baking powder
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 🌱 Vegan Version: Replace butter with 2/3 cup (150g) 🥥 coconut oil and eggs with 6 Tbsp of raw tahini.
  • 🌾 Gluten-Free Version: Use certified gluten-free quick oats.
Let's Do This:
  • Preheat the oven to 165°C (330°F).
  • Melt the butter and dark chocolate together in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until fully combined and smooth.
    2/3 cup (150g) butter, 7oz (200g) dark chocolate
  • Whisk together the eggs, brown sugar, and salt until smooth, then mix in the melted chocolate, oats, and baking powder until everything is well combined.
    2 eggs, 1/3 cup (67g) brown sugar, 2 cups (160g) oats, 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Pour the batter into a parchment-lined baking dish and bake for 12-15 minutes, until the center is slightly gooey.
  • Let the brownies cool down, then place in the fridge for 60 minutes to set.
    Only after chilling, transfer to a cutting board and slice into squares.
