Discover the delicious side of cauliflower with this roasted version, packed with savory spices and a crispy finish—easy to make and a perfect addition to any meal.🌿

Ingredients:

For the Roasted Cauliflower:

  • 🥦 1 head of white cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 🫒 1/4 cup (60 ml) olive oil
  • 🧂 1 tsp salt and pepper (each)
  • 🌶️ 1 tsp paprika
  • 🧀 2 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese

For the Yogurt Sauce:

  • 🍶 1 cup Greek yogurt (or plain yogurt)
  • 🧄 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 🥄 1 tsp mustard
  • 🌿 1 Tbsp dill (fresh or dried, or any herbs you like)
  • 🍋 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper to taste
  • ✨ Optional: Crumbled feta cheese
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 Not feeling the Yogurt sauce? Try a classic Tahini sauce for a unique, flavorful twist!
  • ✨ Add fresh herbs or a little honey for extra flavor!
Let's Do This:
  • 🔥 Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  • 🥄 Prep the cauliflower: Mix olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, and Parmesan. Coat the cauliflower florets.
    1/4 cup (60 ml) olive oil, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 1 tsp paprika, 2 tsps grated Parmesan
  • 🍽️ Roast: Spread the cauliflower on a baking sheet (covered with parchment paper), roast for 25 minutes, then broil uncovered for 10 minutes until golden brown.
  • 🥣 Make the sauce: Mix yogurt, garlic, mustard, herbs, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Stir in feta (optional).
    1 cup Greek yogurt, 1 garlic clove, 1 tsp mustard, dill, 2 Tbsp lemon juice, Salt & pepper
  • ✨ Serve: Spread yogurt sauce on a plate, top with cauliflower or drizzle sauce on top, and enjoy!
