I'll be honest—I was surprised the first time I tried carrot cake. I didn't think I'd like it, but one bite and I was hooked! Now it's one of my absolute faves, and I can't wait for you to fall in love with it too.

Ingredients:

For the Cake:

  • 🥕 3 cups (400g) grated carrots
  • 🌰 1 cup (120g) chopped or ground pecans
  • 🍬 3/4 cup (175g) brown sugar
  • 🍬 1/2 cup (110g) white sugar
  • 🥖 1 3/4 cups (225g) all-purpose flour
  • 🥄 1 tsp baking powder
  • 🧂 1 tsp baking soda
  • 🍂 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 🌱 1/2 cup (125ml) vegetable oil
  • 🍶 1 small cup (125g) Greek yogurt (or regular yogurt)
  • 🥚 2 eggs
  • 🍦 2 tsp vanilla extract

For the Frosting:

  • 🥛 1 cup (250g) cream cheese
  • 🥛 1/2 cup (100g) ricotta cheese
  • 🍚 1/4 cup (55g) powdered sugar
  • 🍋 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 🍨 1 tsp vanilla extract
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 You can use a mixer to speed up the mixing process.
  • 💡 You may use a 22cm (8.66") or two English cake pans.
  • 💡 Grating the carrots is the usual way, but I found that grinding them really enhances the flavor. What do you think—would you give it a try?
Let's Do This:
  • Grate or grind carrots and place them in a large bowl. Grind or chop pecans and add them to the bowl.
    3 cups (400g) carrots, 1 cup (120g) pecans
  • Add all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and both sugars. Stir everything together until well combined.
    1 3/4 cups (225g) all-purpose flour, 1 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, 2 tsp cinnamon, 3/4 cup (175g) brown sugar, 1/2 cup (110g) white sugar
  • Add vegetable oil, Greek yogurt (or regular yogurt), eggs, and vanilla extract to the bowl. Mix until smooth and evenly combined. You can use a mixer to make this step quicker.
    1/2 cup (125ml) vegetable oil, 1 small cup (125g) Greek yogurt, 2 eggs, 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F). Grease your cake pan and line it with parchment paper.
  • Pour the batter into the pan and bake for about 1 hour, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let the cake cool completely.

For the Frosting:

  • Beat together cream cheese, ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until smooth.
    1 cup (250g) cream cheese, 1/2 cup (100g) ricotta cheese, 1/4 cup (55g) powdered sugar, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Once the cake is completely cool, spread the frosting evenly over the top.
