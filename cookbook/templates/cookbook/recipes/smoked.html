Perfectly creamy cream cheese, paired with rich smoked salmon and fresh herbs—this appetizer is quick, easy, and packed with flavor! 😋

Ingredients:

  • 🧀 10.5oz (300g) cream cheese
  • 🐟 4.2oz (120g) smoked salmon
  • 🌿 Fresh dill (or any herb you like)
  • 🌿 Chopped chives (or onion)
  • 🫒 2 tbsp (30ml) olive oil
  • 🍷 1 tbsp (15ml) balsamic vinegar
  • 🫒 capers (optional)
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 For extra crunch, serve with crispy crackers or toasted rustic bread on the side.
  • 💡 If you choose to use onion, soak it in water for a few minutes before chopping to mellow the flavor.
Let's Do This:
  • Spread the cream cheese on a plate or board, creating a smooth layer.
    300g cream cheese
  • Arrange the smoked salmon slices on top of the cream cheese.
    120g smoked salmon
  • Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salmon and cream cheese.
  • Sprinkle cappers, fresh dill, chives or onion on top for flavor and color.
