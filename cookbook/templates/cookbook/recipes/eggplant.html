Smoked eggplant, fresh toppings, endless flavor. Feta or Tahini? You decide!

Ingredients:

  • 🍆 1 whole eggplant
  • 🧅 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 🌿 Handful of parsley, chopped
  • 🧄 1-2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 🍋 Lemon juice (a squeeze!)
  • 🍅 1 tomato, grated
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper (to taste)
  • 🥄 Crumbled feta or Tahini drizzle
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 Plate not looking perfect? No worries—just mix it into a spread and enjoy the flavors!
Let's Do This:
  • Preheat oven to the highest setting. Poke eggplant with a fork and broil for 45-60 minutes, until soft and charred. (Or char on the stove for extra smokiness)
  • Scoop out eggplant flesh and mash on a plate.
  • Top with onions, garlic, parsley, and grated tomato. Add lemon, salt, and pepper.
  • Crumble feta or drizzle tahini on top. Enjoy!
