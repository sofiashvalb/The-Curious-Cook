Ready to take your taste buds on a flavorful ride? This creamy tahini sauce is perfect for dipping, drizzling, or spreading—an easy crowd-pleaser you'll want to make again and again!

Ingredients:

  • 🥛 1 cup Tahini
  • 💧 1 cup room temperature water
  • 🍋 Lemon juice (to taste)
  • 🧄 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 🧂 Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 🌶️ 2 tsp paprika
  • 🌿 Dried or fresh herbs (optional)
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 Adjust the consistency by adding more water for a thinner sauce, or less for a thicker spread.
  • 🍴 This sauce pairs wonderfully with grilled meats, roasted vegetables, or pita bread.
Let's Do This:
  • Start by mixing the water and tahini (water first, so it doesn’t stick to the bowl!).
  • Add lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • Stir in paprika and herbs. Taste and adjust if needed.
  • Perfect for dipping, drizzling, or spreading! 😋
