Shrimp, garlic, butter… all the essentials for a flavor-packed dish. Add some wine and cream, and it’s a game-changer. Pair with crispy bread, and you’ve got the perfect bite.
Who’s ready to make their taste buds dance? ✨

Ingredients:

For the Shrimp:

  • 🍤 16oz (450g) uncooked shrimp
  • 🧄 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 🧂 Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 🌶️ Paprika (optional, for that extra kick)

For the Sauce:

  • 🧈 1 stick of butter
  • 🍷 1 glass of white wine (optional but delicious!)
  • 🍶 1 cup (250ml) heavy cream
  • 🌿 Fresh basil, chives, or dill (your pick!)

For Serving:

  • 🍞 Your fave bread (think crusty, like Georgian bread, focaccia, or rustic bread)
Tips & Suggestions:
  • 💡 If using pre-cooked shrimp, just add it to the sauce at the end and warm it through!
  • 💡 I love serving it straight from the pan. If you prefer plating, warm the plate in the microwave first to keep the dish from cooling too quickly.
Let's Do This:
  • Sauté shrimp in a hot pan with salt, pepper, and paprika until they turn red. Set aside.
  • Melt butter, add minced garlic (don’t burn it!), and pour in wine if using. Let it cook off, then add cream. Season to taste.
  • Add shrimp back into the sauce and cook for another minute. Toss in your choice of herbs.
  • Pop your bread in the oven (180°C / 350°F) for 3-4 minutes to make it crispy.
  • Plate the shrimp, drizzle with sauce, and serve with the warm, crispy bread. Don’t forget to dip! 🤤
